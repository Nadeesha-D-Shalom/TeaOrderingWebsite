About Our Company

Welcome to Ceymos Lanka.

Your gateway to the finest Ceylon tea, spices, and coconut products, straight from the heart of Sri Lanka. Rooted in over 30 years of family tradition, Ceymos is more than a business — it's a legacy. While our Sri Lankan branch has been proudly operating for 1 year, our roots stretch deep, with trusted partners and sister companies in Japan, China.

Sri Lankan Excellence

Our tea is grown in the lush, high-altitude estates of Sri Lanka, handpicked and processed with care to preserve its rich aroma and bold flavor. We combine traditional expertise with modern quality standards to bring you world-class Ceylon tea.
